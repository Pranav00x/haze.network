On most chains, your balance becomes a permanent public record the moment you send.

Make a single payment and anyone can walk the rest of your history — what you hold, who you've paid, what you're worth — for as long as the chain exists, with no way to take it back. For actual money, that's not transparency; it's a standing leak. Haze was built the other way around: the network can prove your payment is valid without ever being told what's inside it.

The idea

The network confirms your payment without learning a thing about it.

There are three things a Haze block never records — and they happen to be the three things that would give you away.

01What you holdEvery balance is a commitment carrying a range proof. The proof shows the spend adds up; the amount itself never appears anywhere on chain.
02Who you paidSpent inputs and their outputs cut through and cancel before the block is even written. The link between two people isn't hidden — it's deleted.
03Who you areNo accounts, no addresses. Owning a coin just means knowing the secret that unlocks it. Nothing stored on chain points back to a person.
Everything a block is checked against
inputs − outputs + fees = 0

That single line is the entire validity rule. When the coins in, the coins out, and the fees all cancel to zero, the money is conserved — and the network verified it without ever being handed an amount, a name, or an address.

What you can actually do

Send without announcing it

Pay anyone with the amount kept between the two of you, and the trail linking you severed the moment it settles.

Trade in your whole wallet

Help keep it running

Stake an output straight from the wallet and start validating. The active set is worked out on chain, so no one has to be trusted to run it.

One Rust engine, from a browser tab to the phone in your pocket.

The core has no native dependencies, so the exact same consensus code compiles down to WebAssembly, Android, iOS, and desktop. Every wallet, on every device, is running identical rules — nothing to trust between them, nothing that behaves differently because of where it happens to run.
